KING CYCLE, saving your time and money.
For students, by students.

King Cycle offers delivery services to students at the University of Warwick. Our aim is to make students’ life easier by delivering whatever they need at their doorstep.
The difference between us and other delivery services is that we have structured not only the aim but also the functioning of this company around the student problematic.

Why are we interested in doing this:
  • Team management skills
  • Gain planning and organization skills
  • Gain customer-relation skills
  • Help improve students’ everyday life on campus
